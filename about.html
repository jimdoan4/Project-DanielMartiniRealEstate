Daniel Martini

Daniel Martini was born in Chattanooga, Tennessee and obtained his MFA in Graphic Design from Georgia's prestigious Georgia State University. Initially, he was a lead merchandizer for the Italian luxury goods powerhouse Fendi. There he increased his knowledge of the luxury market and high-end consumer demographic. Later he enjoyed a successful career in upscale, multi-family property management. In that role, he discovered he was a natural at strategic sales and marketing as he consistently exceeded property occupancy and sales goals.
